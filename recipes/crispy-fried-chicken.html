Crispy Fried Chicken

This is a chicken recipe I stole! I will end up providing a link to the original recipe to give credit at the bottom of the page. I haven't tried this chicken myself but DAMN do I love me some crispy chicken. I would add a few more seasonings but this doesn't seem to be terrible. You should try it and then update me. Chicken is a simple meal and we are but simple creatures and we yearn for it's golden-brown crunch. Try this recipe today!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. MAKE THE BREADING: Combine the flour, paprika, salt, and pepper in a zip-top bag. Seal the bag and shake until well-combined.
  2. Dredge The Chicken: Pour buttermilk into a shallow bowl, then dip the chicken (on both sides) until it's completely soaked in buttermilk. Place the soaked chicken in the bag with flour mixture, seal, and shake well to coat. transfer the coated chicken to a backing sheet, and cover. Allow the flour coating to become a paste-like consistency.
  3. Fry The Chicken: Pour the oil in a skillet and bring it to high heat. Brown The chicken on both side, then reduce the heat, cover the skillet, and cook for about 30 minutes. Remove the cover, return the heat to high and fry until extra crispy. Drain the chicken on paper towels and let cool 15 minutes before serving!
