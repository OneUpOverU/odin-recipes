Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp Honey
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce (all purpose or light soy sauce)
  • 1 tbsp White Vinegar
  • 1 large Garlic Clove (or 2 small)
  • 2 Salmon Fillets, skinless (recipe calls for 6oz/ 200g each)
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional: Seasame Seeds
  • Optional: Finely Sliced Chives or Shallots/Scallions
