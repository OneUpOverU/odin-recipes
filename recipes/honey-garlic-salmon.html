Honey Garlic Salmon

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp Honey
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce (all purpose or light soy sauce)
  • 1 tbsp White Vinegar
  • 1 large Garlic Clove (or 2 small)
  • 2 Salmon Fillets, skinless (recipe calls for 6oz/ 200g each)
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional: Seasame Seeds
  • Optional: Finely Sliced Chives or Shallots/Scallions

    • Steps

    1. Take Salmon out and Dry: 20 minutes before cooking, remove the salmon from the fridge and pat dry. Once done sprinke with salt and pepper
    2. Whisk Sauce: Whisk the soy sauce with the White Vinegar, Honey, and Garlic Clove.
    3. Prep The Pan and Cook: Drizzle oil in a non-stick fry pan and heat over medium high heat. Pleace a salmon in the pan and cook the first side for 3-4 minutes until golden. Turn, then cook the other side for 2-3 minutes or until golden.
    4. Pour Sauce over Salmon: Pour the sauce on the salmon and cook for 1 minute or until the sauce thickens slightly. Check the Side of the salmon to tell how cooked through the middle is.
    5. Let cool and serve: Let the salmon cool for 5 minutes and put onto serving plates.
    6. (Optional)Sprinkle Sesame seeds and Chives/Shalots
